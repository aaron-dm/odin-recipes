Scrambled Eggs

Description

Simple fluffy scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • Large eggs
  • Butter (For every 3 eggs add a tablespoon of butter)

Steps

  1. Melt butter in the mircowave. Let cool for a few minutes
  2. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat
  3. Add eggs, salt and pepper to melted butter. Beat with a whisk for at least 1 minute. Eggs should be frothy
  4. Add egg mixture to the preheated skillet. Use a spatula to stir eggs often. The number of eggs determines how fast the eggs will scramble
  5. Remove scrambled eggs from heat at desired firmness and serve immediately
